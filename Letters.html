Letters

The Seventh Section of the Bible

The Epistles (Letters) were written to early churches and individuals, providing instruction, encouragement, and correction for living out the Christian faith.

Romans

罗马书 / 羅馬書

Author:Paul
Written:Around AD 57
To:The church in Rome
Chapters:16

Key Lessons:

All have sinned and fall short of God's glory, Salvation is through faith in Jesus Christ alone, Justification by faith, Living a transformed life in Christ

1 & 2 Corinthians

哥林多前书 / 哥林多前書 & 哥林多后书 / 哥林多後書

Author:Paul
Written:Around AD 55
To:The church in Corinth
Chapters:16 (1 Cor), 13 (2 Cor)

Key Lessons:

God's wisdom revealed through the Spirit, Unity in the body of Christ, The nature of Christian love (1 Cor 13), Forgiving and loving those who offend you, God's power in our weakness

Galatians

加拉太书 / 加拉太書

Author:Paul
Written:Around AD 49
To:The churches in Galatia
Chapters:6

Key Lessons:

There is only one true Gospel, Freedom in Christ vs. slavery to the law, We are all children of God through faith, The fruit of the Spirit

Ephesians

以弗所书 / 以弗所書

Author:Paul
Written:Around AD 60
To:The church in Ephesus
Chapters:6

Key Lessons:

Living a life in Christ, Unity in the body of Christ, Walking in love and light, Putting on the full armor of God, Christian family relationships

Philippians

腓立比书 / 腓立比書

Author:Paul
Written:Around AD 61
To:The church in Philippi
Chapters:4

Key Lessons:

Joy in all circumstances, Living life worthy of the Gospel, Imitating Christ's humility, Pressing toward the goal in Christ

Colossians

歌罗西书 / 歌羅西書

Author:Paul
Written:Around AD 60
To:The church in Colossae
Chapters:4

Key Lessons:

The supremacy of Christ, Living as those made alive in Christ, Setting minds on things above, The Christian household

1 & 2 Thessalonians

帖撒罗尼迦前书 / 帖撒羅尼迦前書 & 帖撒罗尼迦后书 / 帖撒羅尼迦後書

Author:Paul
Written:AD 51 (1 Thess), AD 52 (2 Thess)
To:The church in Thessalonica
Chapters:5 (1 Thess), 3 (2 Thess)

Key Lessons:

Living to please God, The return of Christ, Comfort in tribulation, Stay away from idleness, Stand firm in faith

1 & 2 Timothy

提摩太前书 / 提摩太前書 & 提摩太后书 / 提摩太後書

Author:Paul
Written:AD 65 (1 Tim), AD 67 (2 Tim)
To:Timothy
Chapters:6 (1 Tim), 4 (2 Tim)

Key Lessons:

The need for prayer and proper worship, Qualifications for church leaders, Dealing with false teachers, Guard the truth, Fight the good fight of faith

Titus

提多书 / 提多書

Author:Paul
Written:Around AD 64
To:Titus
Chapters:3

Key Lessons:

Salvation is based on God's grace and mercy, God's grace empowers believers to live godly lives, Qualifications for church elders

Philemon

腓利门书 / 腓利門書

Author:Paul
Written:Around AD 60
To:Philemon
Chapters:1

Key Lessons:

Christian forgiveness and reconciliation, We are to love others as Christ loves us, Brotherhood in Christ transcends social status

Hebrews

希伯来书 / 希伯來書

Author:Unknown
Written:Around AD 68
To:Hebrew Christians
Chapters:13

Key Lessons:

The supremacy of Christ over all, Jesus as our great High Priest, Faith demonstrated by Old Testament heroes, God disciplines His children, Persevere in faith

James

雅各书 / 雅各書

Author:James (brother of Jesus)
Written:Between AD 45-50
To:Jewish Christians scattered abroad
Chapters:5

Key Lessons:

Faith without works is dead, Loving one another equally without favoritism, Taming the tongue, Wisdom from above, Patience in suffering

1 & 2 Peter

彼得前书 / 彼得前書 & 彼得后书 / 彼得後書

Author:Peter
Written:AD 62-64 (1 Peter), AD 67 (2 Peter)
To:Scattered believers in Asia Minor
Chapters:5 (1 Peter), 3 (2 Peter)

Key Lessons:

Hope in suffering as believers of Christ, Living as God's chosen people, The destruction of false teachers, Growing in Christian maturity

1, 2 & 3 John

约翰一书 / 約翰一書 & 约翰二书 / 約翰二書 & 约翰三书 / 約翰三書

Author:John (the apostle)
Written:Around AD 90
To:Early Christians in house churches
Chapters:5 (1 John), 1 (2 John), 1 (3 John)

Key Lessons:

God is light and love, Walking in truth and love, Assurance of salvation, Loving our neighbor as ourselves, Testing the spirits

Jude

犹大书 / 猶大書

Author:Jude (brother of James & Jesus)
Written:Around AD 65
To:Believers called and loved by God
Chapters:1

Key Lessons:

Contend for the faith, Warning against false teachers, God's judgment and justice, Building yourselves up in faith

Genesis

Written: During 1400s BC
Chapters: 50

Written: During 1400s BC
Chapters: 40

The 10 Plagues, The Exodus from Egypt, Crossing the Red Sea, The Journey in the Wilderness, The Ten Commandments

Leviticus

Written: During 1400s BC

The 5 Main Offerings, Laws of Holy Living, The Day of Atonement, Festivals and Feasts

Deuteronomy

Author: Moses
Written: During 1400s BC
Chapters: 34

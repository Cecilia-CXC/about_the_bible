Books of History

Joshua

约书亚记 / 約書亞記

God's Character

Author: Joshua, Phinehas (Ending)
Written: Around 1390 BC
Chapters: 24

Known Stories:

Israel in the Land of Canaan (The Promised Land), The Fall of Jericho, The 12 Tribes Distribution

Judges

士师记 / 士師記

God's Grace in Preserving People Despite Their Rejection

Author: Possibly Samuel
Written: Around 1000 BC
Chapters: 21

Known Stories:

Deborah and Barak, Gideon and the Midianites, Samson and Delilah, The Cycle of Apostasy

Ruth

路得记 / 路得記

Story of Love & Devotion

Author: Unknown
Written: Around 1000 BC
Chapters: 4

Known Stories:

Ruth's Loyalty to Naomi, Ruth and Boaz, Kinsman-Redeemer, Ancestor of King David

1 & 2 Samuel

撒母耳记上 / 撒母耳記上 & 撒母耳记下 / 撒母耳記下

Beginning of Israel's Monarchy

Author: Unknown
Written: Around 930 BC
Chapters: 31 (1 Samuel); 24 (2 Samuel)

Known Stories:

Samuel's Childhood, Saul Becomes King, David and Goliath, David Amongst the Philistines, David Becomes King

1 & 2 Kings

列王纪上 / 列王記上 & 列王纪下 / 列王記下

The Monarchy of Israel

Author: Unknown
Written: Around 560 BC
Chapters: 22 (1 Kings); 25 (2 Kings)

Known Stories:

Reign of Solomon, Building of the Temple, The Kings of Israel & Judah, Elijah and Elisha, The Fall of Israel and Judah

1 & 2 Chronicles

历代志上 / 歷代志上 & 历代志下 / 歷代志下

God's Covenant with David

Author: Possibly Ezra
Written: Around 430 BC
Chapters: 29 (1 Chronicles); 36 (2 Chronicles)

Known Stories:

King David as the Messianic Ideal, Genealogies, Kings of Judah, Temple Worship

Ezra

以斯拉记 / 以斯拉記

Faithfulness to The Lord

Author: Possibly Ezra
Written: Around 440 BC
Chapters: 10

Known Stories:

The Return of the First Exiles, Rebuilding of the Temple, Ezra's Reforms, Opposition to Rebuilding

Nehemiah

尼希米记 / 尼希米記

God's Protection of His People

Author: Possibly Ezra
Written: Around 430 BC
Chapters: 13

Known Stories:

Nehemiah Rebuilding Jerusalem's Walls, Opposition from Enemies, Renewal of the Covenant, Nehemiah's Leadership

Esther

以斯帖记 / 以斯帖記

God's Sovereign Rule

Author: Unknown
Written: Around 460 BC
Chapters: 10

Known Stories:

Esther Made Queen, Esther Saving the Jews from Haman's Plot, Mordecai's Honor, Origin of Purim Festival

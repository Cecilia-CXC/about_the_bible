The Four Gospels

Matthew

马太福音 / 馬太福音

Jesus, the Son of David Who Establishes the Kingdom of Heaven

Author: Matthew (Levi)
Written: Possibly between AD 60-70
Chapters: 28

Unique Events:

The announcement of Jesus' birth to Joseph by an angel in a dream, The visit of the Magi, The Sermon on the Mount, The Great Commission

Mark

马可福音 / 馬可福音

Jesus, the Son of God Who Suffers to Ransom Others

Author: John Mark
Written: Possibly between AD 55-65
Chapters: 16

Unique Events:

The fleeing of a young man leaving his garment behind, Emphasis on Jesus as a man of action and the suffering servant, The shortest Gospel account

Luke

路加福音 / 路加福音

Jesus, the Savior Seeking the Lost

Author: Luke (Physician)
Written: Around AD 60
Chapters: 24

Unique Stories:

Birth of John the Baptist, The parable of the Good Samaritan, The parable of the Prodigal Son, The repentance of the thief on the cross

John

约翰福音 / 約翰福音

Jesus, the Lamb of God Bringing Eternal Life

Author: John (The Beloved)
Written: Possibly around AD 85-90
Chapters: 21

Unique Stories:

The turning of water into wine at Cana, Jesus and Nicodemus, The woman at the well, The raising of Lazarus, The washing of the disciples' feet

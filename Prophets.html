Prophets

The Fourth Section of the Bible

Includes...

Prophecies of the Messiah and much more

Structure

The Prophets are split into two parts:

  • Major Prophets - 5 long books
  • Minor Prophets - 12 short books

Note: "Major" and "Minor" refer to the length of the books, not their importance!

Major Prophets

Isaiah

以赛亚书 / 以賽亞書

The Message of Warning & Hope

Author: Isaiah
Written: Between 701-681 BC
Chapters: 66
Style: Poetic, apocalyptic, and narrative

Jeremiah

耶利米书 / 耶利米書

Promise of the Renewal of the Covenant Through the Messiah

Author: Jeremiah
Written: Between 626-586 BC
Chapters: 52
Style: Poetic, apocalyptic, and narrative

Lamentations

耶利米哀歌 / 耶利米哀歌

The Voice of Grief We Need to Hear

Author: Possibly Jeremiah
Written: 586 BC
Chapters: 5
Style: Poetic

Ezekiel

以西结书 / 以西結書

Hope of the Messianic King

Author: Ezekiel
Written: Between 593-571 BC
Chapters: 48
Style: Poetic, apocalyptic, and narrative

Daniel

但以理书 / 但以理書

The Meaning of Being God's People

Author: Daniel
Written: Around 535 BC
Chapters: 12
Style: Apocalyptic with narrative parts
